Postponed due to bad weather

Dear Freaks,

We were really looking forward to spending the weekend with you all. Unfortunately, however, we have to inform you that the seti has to be canceled due to the current weather forecast. The German weather service is expecting 20-80l/sqm2 of rain.
Under these circumstances, we cannot guarantee the safety of all visitors. After hours of plenary sessions, we have decided that unfortunately we have no other choice.

We understand that this news comes very spontaneously and is disappointing for everyone and we share your feelings. Please understand that we had to make this decision to protect everyone.

However, we also have good news: there will definitely be an alternative date in August. We will let you know more details in the next days. All tickets already purchased will remain valid. If you are unable to attend on this date, please let us know. In this case we will refund the ticket price. Please be patient for a few days and let us sort things out. We will post all informations regarding refunds and the alternative date on Monday.
Thank you very much for your understanding and support. We hope to see you all in the summer.

Your Seti orga team

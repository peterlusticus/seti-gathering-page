About

SETI-Gathering is a festival which brings earthlings together to have liberating and beautiful experiences, be it on the dancefloor immersed in visual and acoustic delights or enjoying the location and delving into art, conversations and other revelations of life. We are a group of friends driven by the wish to create a festival focused on the darker and more experimental side of Psy- and Tekno-culture. We aim to create a space in which we can all freely express ourselves, and shed the layers of our daily social, physical and psychological demands. We would like to invite you to join us on our next edition of SETI to launch ourselves into this years highly anticipated festival-season.

SETI 2k25
31.5.-2.6.

This years edition of SETI-Gathering will happen on the 9th of august. We will make a 48h party in a beautiful location close to Erfurt. We will host 2 Dance floors with mostly dark psychedelic and teknoid dance music as well as blissfull chillout sets. Additionally there will be freshly made food to enjoy and art features placed around the location.

location
Seti-gathering 2025 will take place in a beautiful location near Erfurt. Many of you will allready know it. If you come by train, get out at the station Erfurt Ost. If you come by car, please park your car next to the trainstation. We need to keep the street to the festival free. The coordinates are: 51.013867791211,11.071330958397

Accessibility of the festivalarea:
Unfortunately, the festival area is difficult to access with a wheelchair. Almost every area is accessible without stairs, but the ground is very bumpy. Additionally, we cannot provide barrier-free toilets.

Food and Drinks:
We will have food shops at our gathering as well as free drinking water. Please use this resource mindfully, as we are not connected to the water grid. Please get water for cleaning or water guns from tanks that aren't labeled as drinking water.
camping
There will be a limited Camping space. Please pack wisely, so everyone can have a comfortable space for camping. Also pack something to protect you from the sun, as there is no shadow on the camping ground. The SETI is surrounded by nature reserves and agricultural land, so wild camping can't be tolerated. Farmers and their vehicles need a lot of space around the roads, which have to be accessible all the time. Also you don't camp in nature reserves.

Showers:
There is a single outdoor shower that you may use.
awareness
To ensure a safe experience for everyone, we will have an awarenessteam, which will be recognizable by string lights. If you or others feel harassed or you see something suspicious, please do not hesitate and ask the awarenessteam for help. SETI Gathering is no place for hate, sexism, racism, queerphobia or any other form of disrespectfull behaviour. Our staff is sensitized to recognize scene clothing and codes.

Medication:
If you need to bring medication that requires refrigeration, you can leave it at the bar. The medication will be stored safely and handed to you as needed

Light effects:
This year, the Arecibo stage will be free from fast strobing lights. There will be general ambient lighting and projection mapping with beamers and lasers. The greenhouse floor will have strobing and disorienting lights throughout.

If you have any questions or notes regarding accessibility topics, feel free to contact us.
